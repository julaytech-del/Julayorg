Cookie Policy

Effective date: 13 April 2026  ·  Last updated: 13 April 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website places on your device — computer, phone, or tablet — when you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

Cookies set by the website you are visiting are called first-party cookies. Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called third-party cookies. Julay uses first-party cookies only; we do not embed third-party advertising or tracking scripts.

Cookies may be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored for a defined period or until you delete them).

Cookies we use

The table below lists every cookie Julay sets, grouped by purpose category. We keep this list up to date whenever we add or remove cookies.

Strictly Necessary

These cookies are required for the service to function. You cannot opt out of them while using Julay. They do not store any personally identifiable information beyond what is needed for authentication and security.

Cookie name Purpose Duration Party
julay_session Maintains your authenticated session across page loads. Session 1st party

julay_csrf A cross-site request forgery (CSRF) token that helps protect form submissions from malicious attacks. Session 1st party

Functional

Functional cookies remember choices you make to personalise your experience. They are not strictly necessary, but disabling them may affect how the app looks and behaves.

Cookie name Purpose Duration Party
julay_lang Saves your language preference so you are presented with the correct locale on each visit. 1 year 1st party
julay_theme Remembers your UI theme selection (light, dark, or system default). 1 year 1st party

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our marketing site. Plausible is a privacy-first, EU-hosted analytics provider.

✓ Plausible Analytics does not use cookies. It collects fully anonymised, aggregate statistics without setting any cookies on your device and without processing any personally identifiable information.

The one analytics-adjacent cookie we set is your consent record:

Cookie name Purpose Duration Party
julay_consent_v1 Records your cookie consent choices so we do not show the consent banner on every page view. 1 year 1st party

Marketing

We currently use no marketing or advertising cookies. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and, where required by law, request your consent before setting any such cookies.

Our consent banner

When you first visit julay.org, a consent banner is displayed that lets you accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your choice is saved in the julay_consent_v1 cookie for one year. You can change your preferences at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.

Strictly necessary cookies are set regardless of your consent because they are essential for the operation of the service. Functional and analytics cookie categories are only activated after you give explicit consent, unless applicable law permits otherwise.

For full details on how we process personal data and your rights under applicable data protection law, please read our Privacy Policy.
