About Julay

Founded in 2026 to change how teams work with AI.

Julay was founded in 2026 with a clear conviction: AI-powered project management should not be a privilege reserved for large enterprises with dedicated tooling budgets. Every team — a two-person startup, a school department, a creative agency — deserves intelligent software that reduces busywork and amplifies human judgment.

We built Julay to be the operating layer for modern work: one workspace where your projects, tasks, documents, and AI assistants live together and learn from each other. No more stitching together a dozen apps. No more losing context as work moves between tools.

We are a fully distributed team. Our investors, advisors, and early customers span multiple continents, and we are proud to build a product that works for teams regardless of where they are in the world.

Why Julay?

We believe great work happens when intelligent tooling gets out of your way.

⚡

AI at the core

AI is not a bolt-on feature in Julay — it is built into the data model. Every project, task, and document is AI-aware from the moment it is created.

🔓

Privacy by design

Your work data is yours. We do not train models on your content, and we never sell your data. Privacy and security are non-negotiable defaults.

⚖

Built for every team

From solo founders to growing companies, Julay scales with you. Start free, grow without friction, and never pay for features you do not use.

Our mission

What we are here to do.

"To make AI-powered project management accessible, trustworthy, and genuinely useful for every team on earth."

We measure success not by the sophistication of our AI features, but by the reduction in friction experienced by the teams who trust us with their work. When Julay is at its best, you stop thinking about the software and start thinking about the work.
